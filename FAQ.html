02

How Do I Book A Flight?

Currently, you cannot book a seat on any flights through SpaceFlight. We hope to change this in the coming years as commercial spaceflight becomes more common and opens up to the wider, general public - just as international flights did.
Until then, we have summarised the steps you need to take in order to get that all-important ticket to space:

  1. Firstly, read all of our FAQs to ensure you have all the information, and meet all of the necassary requirements to go to space.
  2. Then, visit our Upcoming Flights page to research the current providers offering commercial spaceflight and which one best fits you.
  3. On the same page you can see when the next available flights are scheduled for, (so you can take time off work!) and see if there is any training required for example.
  4. Finally, keep an eye on our News page to be the first one to book your space on the next launch.
